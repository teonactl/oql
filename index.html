Everything you need on stage

Built for sound engineers, stage managers and operators working under pressure.

🎵

Multi-track audio

Playback of WAV, FLAC and MP3 with configurable fade in/out, loop, slices, volume automation and L/R routing.

🎬

Video Cue

Project video to a secondary monitor in sync with the cue list. Supports H.264, ProRes and all Qt Multimedia formats.

🔊

Fade & playback control

Dedicated cues for volume fade, stop, pause, play and real-time rate changes on any active audio source.

🎙

Live microphone

Mic Cue opens a live audio input during the show with full routing and plugin chain support.

📝

On-screen text

Display lyrics, subtitles or cue notes on a secondary monitor with customisable font, colour and alignment.

🔌

LV2 & VST2 plugins

Full plugin chain on every Audio Cue. Native LV2 and VST2 support for real-time audio processing.

🌍

Multilingual

UI available in English, Italian, Spanish and French. Switch language at any time from settings.

🌐

Remote web control

Built-in web server lets you trigger GO from a browser on any tablet or phone on the same network.

⚡

Auto-continue & follow

Chain cues in automatic sequences with configurable pre-wait and post-wait. Perfect for complex unattended shows.
